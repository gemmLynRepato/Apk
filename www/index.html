DEFINITIONS

 Mean: The average value of the entire set of numbers.

 Median: The middle value between the smallest and largest in a set of numbers.

 Mode: The number which appears most often in a set of numbers.

HOW TO SOLVE

Mean
  1. Add all of the numbers in the set.
  2. Count the amount of numbers in the set.
  3. Divide the sum of the numbers by the amount of numbers.
Median
  1. Sort all of the numbers in the set from least to greatest.
  2. If the count of numbers is odd, the median is the middle number.
  3. If the count of numbers is even, the median will be the mean of the two central numbers.
Mode
  1. Find the number that occurs the most.
